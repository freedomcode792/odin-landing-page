Meet the Maids!

Nijiura Maids are a bunch of made-up characters originating from Futaba Channel--a japanese imageaboard. Each maid has her own distinct personality and appearance, as well as a pun name implying what the maid is all about. These maids are fairly popular in narrow circles even outside Japan, and have inspired a lot of art and music, even creating a genre of the latter: maidcore.
